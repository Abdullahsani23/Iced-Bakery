Life is short. Eat cake!

Iced Bakery is locally owned and operated by Sara Bates in Marion, Iowa. Iced Bakery is the leading made-to-order bakery in the corridor
and serves Marion, Cedar Rapids, Hiawatha, Iowa City, Central City, Center Point, Amana, and surrounding areas. With over 15 years of experience,
Iced Bakery specializes in sculpted cakes, custom wedding cakes, gourmet cupcakes, and designer cookies. Other specialty dessert items,
including cake pops, pies, and baked goods, may be available upon request.


Not sure where to start with your upcoming event? Schedule a consultation with Iced Bakery. We can discuss your event theme, timeline,
and logistics to help make your event turn out sweet! Iced Bakery does offer catering options as well as delivery and set up for most events.


Iced Bakery is open Monday-Friday from 10AM to 5PM and Saturday from 10AM to 1PM. Sundays are reserved for special order consultations
only. Iced Bakery will work to accommodate after hours by appointment.
